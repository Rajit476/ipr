1 Problem Analysis (Current Healthcare Issues)

The healthcare system faces critical challenges that compromise patient safety and treatment efficiency:

  • Fragmented health records – Many hospitals store patient data separately, causing errors.
  • Medication errors – Doctors may not see full patient history or prescriptions.
  • Identity verification failures – Patients can be misidentified due to manual ID systems.
  • Limited access to patient data – Doctors waste time retrieving records.
  • Healthcare worker overload – Doctors spend ~80% of time on administrative tasks.

2 Root Causes

These problems exist due to fundamental systemic issues:

  • No unified national Electronic Health Record (EHR) system
  • Manual identity verification processes
  • Siloed hospital databases
  • No real-time conflict detection for drugs/allergies
  • No biometric authentication system

Impact:

These factors lead to data fragmentation and treatment mistakes that could be prevented with proper integration.

5 Key Activities

Core operational activities that ensure HealthSync delivers secure, reliable healthcare services:

  • Patient Data Management: Securely collecting, storing, and organizing patient records to ensure information is accurate and accessible
  • Biometric Authentication: Implementing secure login methods, like fingerprint or facial recognition, to verify the identity of healthcare providers and patients
  • Prescription Safety: Using automated checks to prevent medication errors, such as dangerous drug interactions or incorrect dosages
  • Alert & Notification: Sending real-time updates to users regarding medication schedules, appointment reminders, or urgent health changes
  • System Maintenance: Regularly updating software and hardware to ensure the platform remains functional, fast, and bug-free
  • Compliance: Ensuring all operations strictly follow healthcare laws and data privacy regulations like HIPAA or GDPR

6 HealthSync Solution

The system proposes a Unified Biometric Health Record Platform with comprehensive features:

  • Secure instant data access for hospitals and doctors

This creates a single, secure patient identity linked to all medical records across the entire healthcare network.
